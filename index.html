Who we are

We’re a student team building one place to ask quick questions without bouncing between departments.

Mission

Reduce friction for students who need fast, reliable direction for campus services.

Principles

  • Clear answers with sources and nesssary steps
  • best opitions
  • Escalation to department support if nesssary

Scope

General student FAQs, service navigation, and common process guidance for the demo.

What we do

Ask a question → get an answer → get next steps (links, actions, and the right office to contact).

Answer questions

Courses, forms, deadlines, services, and common “where do I go?” issues.

Route correctly

Points to the correct support page/office and what info you need before contacting them.

Escalate when needed

If the bot isn’t confident, it suggests the correct human support path.
